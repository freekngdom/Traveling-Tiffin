Traveling Tiffin creates delicious, healthy food and educates people how to make healthy, informed eating decisions. Using local, seasonal ingredients, Traveling Tiffin is focused on food that heals. We strive to deliver the right food at the right time, be it the specific needs of a paleo marathoner or a vegan raver, we have the meal to recover and nourish you. The focus is healthy, local, organic and delicious food that heals the body and soothes the mind. We cater events and festivals, and host evenings of food and marijuana pairings.
